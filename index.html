Current Opportunities

Web Designer

Full-time Entry-level Permanent Remote Optional

As a Junior Web Designer at Hot Beans Web, you’ll contribute to both the visual and functional aspects of web pages. Your work will help shape the design, layout, and user experience of our websites, while ensuring they are responsive and accessible across devices. While web design focuses on layout and user experience, web development involves programming the site’s functionality. Though the two roles often overlap, this position is primarily focused on front-end design. With the increasing variety of devices and screen sizes, your ability to create responsive, mobile-friendly designs is essential. You’ll be involved in testing and refining websites throughout their development lifecycle to ensure a smooth and consistent user experience. You will collaborate closely with developers and other creatives to bring ideas to life. We’re looking for a creative and enthusiastic individual to join our growing team of developers and designers here at Hot Beans Web.

Salary

  • £24,000 - £30,000 per annum depending on experience.
  • Bonus scheme
  • Performance Bonus
  • Yearly Bonus

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate on a wide range of design projects for different audiences and clients
  • Create visually appealing and effective digital designs and website layouts
  • Turn design concepts into functional user interfaces in partnership with developers
  • Build responsive web designs that adapt across various devices and screen sizes
  • Develop wireframes, user flows, and page mockups for client approval
  • Participate in design discussions and present ideas to team members and clients
  • Support the maintenance and visual updates of existing websites

Required Skills:

  • Proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator or other visual design and wire-framing tools
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS and JavaScript for rapid prototyping
  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum development process
  • Excellent visual design skills with sensitivity to user-system interaction
  • Familiarity with version control systems (Git)
  • Proven experience as a Digital / Web Designer – ideally with marketing agency experience. A portfolio of work will be required pre-interview.
